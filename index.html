Avatar: The Last Airbender

Overview

Avatar: The Last Airbender is an Emmy award-winning American animated television series that aired for three seasons on Nickelodeon and the Nicktoons Network. Avatar is set in an Asian-influenced world of martial arts and elemental manipulation. The show drew on elements from East Asian, South Asian, and Western culture, making it a mixture of what were previously traditionally separate categories of Japanese anime and Western domestic cartoons.

The series follows the adventures of the main protagonist Aang and his friends, who must save the world by defeating Fire Lord Ozai and ending the destructive war with the Fire Nation.
